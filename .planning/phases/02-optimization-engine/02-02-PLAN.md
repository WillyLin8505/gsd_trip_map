## Phase Goal

**As a** developer calling the optimization engine, **I want to** turn an ordered route plus per-place opening hours and durations into a day-by-day schedule with concrete arrival and departure times, **so that** I receive a feasible itinerary that never books a closed place and never silently drops a place.
